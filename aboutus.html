About Us

Science Zone's ideal roots were implanted in the house of our director Ankit Vijayvargiya with mere 4 students. Starting exclusively with CBSE + JEE coaching, it diversified to State board coaching along with State and National Level Entrance Examinations for Engineering and Medical.


This institute is not an institute but a family who nurtures its student to being a better human being along with higher intelligence. The institute is spearheaded by Ankit Vijayvargiya and Ashish Jajoo both are friends started with a thought to spread the education to students like parental guidance.

Ankit Vijayvargiya is Msc.B.Ed in Maths and Ashish Jajoo is B.tech in Electrical Engineering and teach Physics. Our teachers are committed to bringing the best in the child.

Our missions

Science Zone provides an end to end solution for its students. They come into our institute when they are searching for their career pathway and walk out of here ready for their professional degree.

We guide the students till their first day of Engineering/Medical/Pharmacy/Agricultural etc. We believe that each child is unique and needs personalized attention to succeed in this competitive world. We strive hard for not only the educational upliftment of the child, but also for his holistic growth.

Highlights

We provide great class experience with fun and sincerity.

We have been producing Merit rankers along with Jee Qualifiers.

We have accomplished an astonishing growth from mere 6 students to hundreds of students in just two years.
